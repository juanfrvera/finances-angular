Welcome!

With this app you can have all your different money concerns in the same place:

  • Accounts: see the balance of your different bank accounts.
  • Debts: see what debts you need to pay and which persons are in debt with you.
  • Services: load the services you pay each month and keep track of their prices.
  • Totals: keep track of the sum of all the money you have and see graphics of the percentage of it you have on each account.
